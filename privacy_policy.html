Privacy Policy for Quick Math Duel

Last Updated: December 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Quick Math Duel! This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Quick Math Duel is designed to work offline and stores the following information locally on your device using SharedPreferences:

  • Game Scores: Your top 3 scores for each game mode (Solo, Duel, Survival)
  • Game Settings: Your preferences for sound, vibration, and mute settings
  • Language Preference: Your selected app language
  • Onboarding Status: Whether you have completed the initial onboarding
Important: All this data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third parties. You can clear this data at any time by uninstalling the app.

Data Collected by Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

Our App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. When you interact with ads, Google may collect certain information according to their Privacy Policy. This may include:

  • Device identifiers (such as Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Device information (model, operating system)
  • App usage data related to ad interactions

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • AdMob Privacy Policy

You can opt out of personalized ads by adjusting your device's ad settings:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising > Limit Ad Tracking

How We Use Information

Local Data

  • Game scores and settings are used solely to enhance your gaming experience within the App
  • Language preference is used to display the App in your chosen language
  • No local data is shared with third parties

AdMob Data

  • AdMob uses collected data to serve relevant advertisements
  • We do not have access to or control over the data collected by AdMob
  • Ad personalization helps support the free availability of our App

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  1. Google AdMob: As described above, AdMob may collect data for advertising purposes
  2. Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights

Children's Privacy

Quick Math Duel is designed to be family-friendly and suitable for children. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • The App does not require account creation or login
  • All data is stored locally on the device
  • Parents can delete all data by uninstalling the App

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  1. Access: You can view your game scores and settings within the App
  2. Deletion: You can delete all data by uninstalling the App or using the "Clear History" feature in the App. For detailed instructions, visit our Data Deletion Request Page
  3. Control: You can control ad personalization through your device settings
  4. Opt-Out: You can opt out of personalized ads as described above

Data Security

  • All game data is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms
  • We do not transmit your game data over the internet
  • We do not maintain servers or databases that store your information

Third-Party Links

Our App may contain links to third-party websites or services (such as Google's privacy policies). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

International Users
